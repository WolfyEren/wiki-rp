Valentine

Full Name: Valentine Albane (Used Sugar as a last name alias)

Nicknames: Val (Shortened Name), Valen-tin can (me)

Age: 23

Race: Werewolf (Formally Human)

Title: The Detective

Current Appearance: Red Hair with Green Eyes, Tan Leather Coat with Blue tie; Gray Sweater, White Fur Color

Past Appearance: Red Buttoned Blazer with Black Pleated Skirt (School Uniform), Red Varsity Jacket (From Mother)

Mask Visage: A White Wolf Mask

Hobbies: Meddling into personal affairs to solve cases (except for that one time when eren was like, dead for 3 years)

Skills: Observant, Self Sufficient

Strengths: Voice of Reason, Can be entirely focused and dedicated to a goal (Spends an entire night trying to search for Eren after he ran away)

Weaknesses: Often blinded by her own ambition (Afraid to help Eren out of fear of losing all her hard work towards getting her job, Ends up framing her friends for murder), Has a hard time expressing herself and may pass off as inconsiderate

Biography

A confident detective who believes in her skills and abilities.

Relationships

Eren

Eren is Valentine's closest friend since her childhood. They met when she stuck up for Eren against kids that were bullying him for being a werewolf. Since then they've maintained a good friendship up until graduation when Eren somehow went missing. Val was however too busy with trying to pursue her career as a Private Investigator to notice that he went missing (Ironic). She instead thought that Eren had to move or some situation had come up where he couldn't remain in contact with her anymore. 5 years later, much to her surprise she ends up reuniting with Eren after her mail got sent to the wrong Sugar as both her and Eren were overjoyed to see eachother again, both not knowing what really happened to each other. (More to come later)

Sugar

Sugar is a good friend of Valentine. She oftens confides to her for help with any problems she has with Eren, most notablely when she feels bad for "betraying" Eren.

Luna

Luna is an unknown mentioned close friend to Val and Eren. Supposedely lost contact.

Landon

Valentine doesn't really care for Landon as he oftens antagonizes Eren which she dislikes and gets mad at him about.
