5 Year Anniversary

Today marks the 5 year anniversary of the death of Marie Twilight, who was 9 at the time of her murder. The suspect was never found, but we hope he gets karma one day. Her parents, Karin Twilight and Harold Twilight have reportedly tried to sell their pizzeria, Freddy Fazbear’s Pizza after the loss of their daughter. From the scandal of the death, no companies wish to buy the franchise. It is reported that the co-owner and financial manager of the pizzeria William Afton has made a speech about Marie, who he was quite fond of. His speech is as follows:

“I feel so sorry for Harold, his daughter was one of the only things that made his life happy. The news of her death shook us both severely, I was quite fond of Marie. When she’d sit at the pizzeria and watch us work, I could tell she wanted to do it with us. It’s such a shame that she never got that chance. I hope she rests well in her afterlife, she left us too soon.”
