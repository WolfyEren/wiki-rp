Final Fantasy ReVisit

Overview/Planning

Eren the Dragoon Bangaa, Sugar the X moogle, Valentine the Ravager Gria, Landon the... X Hume. Eren disappears and the group finds the board game in his room spread out (assuming he went back in there willingly). They remake their characters and go in after him. This time each character has a specific element tied to them (except for Landon). Eren is Ice, Valentine is Fire (opposites attract, platonically), Sugar is Lightning. No more spoiling it.
