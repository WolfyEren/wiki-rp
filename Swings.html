Cliffside Swings

Overview

A Swingset that was (re)built near the edge of a cliffside by Eren and Valentine. This was their favorite childhood spot despite the dangers and will still hangout out there together whenever they get the time.

Layout

Kind of self explanatory. A swingset on the edge of a cliffside overlooking the sea with a great view of the sunset. For those platonic moments.
