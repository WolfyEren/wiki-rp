Min

"People..misunderstand me a lot, like how I'm feeling. So..anger is the most obvious thing to do, just..be mad..then they all decided to be mad back. I.... hate being misunderstood..." -Min to Eren

Full Name: Min Kween

Nicknames: Queen

Age: 22

Race: Full Snow Leopard Mystic (Formally Human)

Title: The Mystic Chef

Current Appearance: Black Messy Hair with Yellow Eyes, Chef's Uniform (Work), Black Zippered Jacket with a white top while wearing black shorts (Casual), White Fur with Black/Brown Spots

Hobbies: Swearing

Skills: Can cook, Ruthless in combat, Singing/Vocals, threatening to pretty much everyone (even the animatronics, including ST)

Strengths: Is straightforward and direct

Weaknesses: Has anger issues, Hard time with Self control (Almost kills the dayshift guard and had to held back and calmed down by Eren)

Likes: Being Alone, Relaxing, Being treated normally

Dislikes: Being Misunderstood, annoying people, pranks

Biography

A chef working at Pizzeria for X years. She was having a normal day until one of the day guards started to tease and annoy her (for plot of course). While arguing and fighting with the Day guard, she almost mauled his face off. Quickly being stopped by Eren, she is calmed down and convinced to live with them for free rent and now she works at the Pizzeria as a snow leopard.

Relationships

Eren

Min's Co-Boss. Despite her anger issues and death threats, Eren does his best to calm her down which works. He even offered her a place to stay for free which works for her. When Min's clothes were mysteriously covered in glitter, Eren offered to take off each piece of glitter before Min could murder somebody, to which she was thankful for. They share a heart to heart conversation afterwards where she opens to up to him about how she hates being understood, Eren promised that he'll be there for her as a friend since he's gone through the same thing as well. He tries to stick up for her where he can but there comes a point where force will have to be used.

Sugar

Min's Boss. They have a rather strict professional relationship but Sugar does stand up for her and confronts the chefs about their remarks towards her. When being told this by Eren, she agrees to bake a cake for Sugar's bday, even singing happy birthday while presenting the cake. This gets them much more acquainted with each other.

Jenny

A frequent target of pranks by "The Dasterdly Jenny" because she gives the best reactions. Min is getting fed up with it, especially now with Jay helping her out.

Jay

Another person with the 'ay' suffixes that annoys her, especially when working with another person that annoys her.

Day

A dayguard that pissed off the wrong chef. Was almost murdered until Eren intervened. (He's not getting a wiki page)
