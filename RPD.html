Replay Police Department

Overview

The Police Department in the Northern District of Replay. Valentine, Jackson, Sandy, Sallie, and Chief work here.

Layout

Not the whole layout.

The Camera Room, Locker Rooms, and Courtyard is down "The Hallway".

The Holding Cells and Interrogation Rooms are down the West Hallway.
