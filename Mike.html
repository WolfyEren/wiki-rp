Biography

Close friend to Sugar as a kid, was abused by his father in an attempt to make him the perfect heir for Fazbears. Ended up losing contact with his father and trying to track him and the others down. Eventually did.

Relationships (Based on Strongest)

Sugar (Positive)

Best friend (almost brother) to Sugar, both before and after her death. Helps her as much as he can, is grateful she keeps him around.

Eren (Positive)

Hopefully friend to him, likes his company. Isn't too sure how to talk about their past with him though.

Landon (Neutral)

Only knows him as Sugar's boyfriend. Promised him he wouldn't hurt her.

William Afton (Negative)

Despises his father for the pain he's put so many people through, including himself. Is slightly annoyed he couldn't kill him, but is thankful that Sugar took care of him.
